NT-proBNP

Wed, 03/17/2010
It is incumbent upon those who set underwriting practices for life insurers to do so in a manner which best accommodates the broad mandates of senior management as they relate to risk appraisal. This paper, building upon the evidence presented in its 2007 predecessor, makes an overwhelming argument for using NT-proBNP as both a screening and reflexive test. It is also clearly shown that NT-proBNP should replace routine screening with resting electrocardiograms and treadmill tests.
